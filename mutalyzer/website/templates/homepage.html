Mutalyzer is an HGVS variant nomenclature checker

The aim of this program suite is to support checks of sequence variant nomenclature according to the guidelines of the Human Genome Variation Society.

Examples: AB026906.1:c.274G>T, NM_012337.1:c.274Gdel, NG_012337.1(SDHD_v001):c.274G>T

Generate HGVS

Allows you to generate the HGVS variant description from a reference sequence and an observed sequence.

Webservices

Most Mutalyzer functionality is programmatically available through two interfaces: a SOAP webservice and an HTTP/RPC+JSON webservice.
